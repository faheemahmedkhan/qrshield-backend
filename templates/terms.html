2. Service Description and Purpose

QRShield utilizes cutting-edge Deep Learning (CNN) algorithms and Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XGBoost + SHAP) to estimate the threat vector associated with physical QR code structures and URL lexicons.

The platform provides probabilistic security assessments, not absolute guarantees. Attackers constantly evolve their obfuscation techniques. We strictly advise against relying solely on QRShield's Fusion Score to authorize highly-sensitive operations.
